Commenting Bingo

A fan challenge to read, connect, and spread a little love.

The Mission

This event exists to bolster community between like-minded fans of violetshipping, encourage new connections and friendships, practice and build a habit for comment writing, support fellow fans and creatives — and most of all, to make someone, including yourself, smile.

How It Works

How to Play

01

Request a bingo card. Choose your size and which category squares you want included.

02

Find a violetshipping fanfic that fits a square's category. Read it and leave a comment.

03

Mark off the square. Cover a line vertically, horizontally, or diagonally for BINGO — or fill the whole card for a BLACKOUT!

Questions

FAQ

What kind of comments should I write?

Something nice and positive! A few sentences telling the author what you enjoyed is a great start. There's no minimum or maximum length — we're all adults, and no one is checking your work. This is your chance to connect with someone who decided to share their creativity with you.

If a fic doesn't vibe with you, find something positive you liked — or back out and find another. Constructive criticism is highly discouraged unless the author has explicitly invited it.

Help — I don't know what to say!

Commenting is a learned skill and it gets easier with practice. Here are some resources to help:

  • Why comment? Investing in your community
  • "Every Comment Counts" infographic
  • 101 Comment Starters
  • The trick to leaving amazing comments (for nervous commenters)
  • Fanfiction commenting guide
  • @longlivefeedback's comment builder spreadsheet
What fics can I read?

Any fic that primarily focuses on Kaiba and Jounouchi's relationship and fits the category of a square you're filling. No restrictions on rating, length, or platform — AO3, FF.net, Tumblr, Twitter/X, and other language archives all count.

You are responsible for curating your own reading experience. Be aware of your triggers and squicks and avoid fics accordingly. (How to search AO3 tags →)

One fic per square?

Ideally yes — one fic and one comment per square, to spread the love to as many writers as possible. You can re-read and re-comment on a fic you've already commented on, though seeking out new fics is encouraged.

Do I have to report my comments or submit proof?

No. This challenge runs entirely on the honor system. If you say you've filled a square, it's filled. You're encouraged (but not required) to rec any fics you enjoyed — sharing the love benefits everyone.

What if I don't finish my card?

No problem at all! Life happens. This is meant to be a fun challenge, not another source of stress. You don't need to report in if you choose not to finish.
