# General Info

*Purpose*: Bolster community between like-minded fans of violetshipping. Encourage new connections/friendships. Practice and build a habit for comment writing. Support your fellow fans/creatives. Make someone and hopefully yourself smile.

*How to play*: Fill in your bingo card by reading and commenting on a violetshipping fanfiction that fulfills a square's category. Cover squares in a line vertically, horizontally, or diagonally for a BINGO victory. Cover every square on your card for a BLACKOUT victory.

# Formats

Nano card: 1 square x 3 squares, no "Free space" square  
Mini card: 3 squares x 3 squares with "Free space" in the center  
Standard card: 5 squares x 5 squares with "Free space" in the center

# Categories

All categories are purposefully broad so that participants can apply the criteria as liberally as they choose to the fics they are comfortable reading and commenting on. Participants will have the chance to curate their selection of possible squares before asking for a randomly generated bingo card, i.e., if you only want SFW squares, you can request the "Smut/PWP" category be excluded for you.

# FAQ

## How many cards can I request?

There are no limits on how many cards you can request of each type.

It's recommended that you try and complete one card first before requesting additionals, though.

## What kind of comments should I write?

Something nice and positive! Generally, a few sentences telling the author what you enjoyed about their story is a good start. There is no minimum or maximum length your comment must be. We're all adults, and no one is checking your work. This is your chance to connect with someone who decided to share their creativity with you!

That being said, remember the old adage: "if you have nothing nice to say, don't say anything at all." We acknowledge not every fic with vibe with every reader, and readers have personal preferences about what they read as much as writers have preferences for what they write. Neither side is wrong. The polite thing to do in such a case is to find something positive you liked about their story. And if you can't, please back button out of the fic and find another where you can.

Constructive criticism is also **highly** discouraged unless the author has preemptively stated they are open to receiving any. Constructive criticism has its place, but it is not usually at the stage where someone has already published and shared their work with you.

## What kind of fics can I read?

Any fic as long as it focuses primarily on Kaiba and Jounouchi's relationship and it fits the category of a bingo square you're filling out. No restrictions on ratings, length, or where the fic is published. You can read on AO3, FF.net, tumblr, twitter/X, and other language archives.

That said, you are also responsible for curating your own reading experience. Be aware of your own triggers and squicks and avoid fics accordingly.

How to search AO3 tags for fics https://archiveofourown.org/admin_posts/10851

## How many fics do I have to read and comment on?

Ideally, one fic/comment = one square. We want to spread the love as much as possible, to as many writers as possible.

## Do I have to report what fics I read or submit my comments?

No.

This challenge operates on the honor system. If you claim you've filled in a square on your bingo card, then it's filled in.

If you wish to rec any of the fics you've read or commented on, you are highly encouraged to share the love with your fellow fans. Maybe they will also discover new gems they didn't know before!

## Can I re-read or comment on a fic I've already commented on before?

Yes.

While you are highly encouraged to seek out new fics you might not have read before, a comment is still a comment and you'll make someone's day.

## What if I don't finish my bingo card?

No problem! Life happens. This is intended to be a fun challenge, not another source of stress in your life. You don't have to report in if you choose not to finish your card(s).
